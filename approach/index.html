What is AppRoach?

It's a bright Saturday morning in October, and all your rock climbing gear is pre-packed into your Tacoma from the night before. You grab coffee, and overnight oats from the fridge, and hop on into your truck, ready to enjoy a beautiful day out on the rocks.

Except... where are you going? You know the crag is down the canyon, but how far? Is there a parking lot, or are you supposed to pull off to the side?

Besides, when you finally arrive, where's the trail? Someone told you there were cairns along the way, but the wind must have knocked it over. Someone else told you about a giant tree as a landmark, but the foliage prevents you from seeing it from the road.

Well, intrepid climber, AppRoach was built with you in mind. We understand that trails and travel can be tricky, and AppRoach has tons of videos and visual guides to get you where you're looking to climb!

Sign Up Process

The sign up process is free, simple, and provides a way to link your AppRoach account across devices.

Sometimes, planning your next climbing trip starts at home, on the computer, but sometimes you need to pivot in the middle of the day. Your account sign up makes sure you stay automatically synced between all your devices.

If ever you switch to a new phone in the future, your preferences stay with your account, and get imported when you login!

To sign up, AppRoach supports creating an account via Gmail, via Apple ID, or via any email account.

Getting Around AppRoach

Welcome to the landing page for AppRoach! This is where you'll find yourself dropped once you sign in.

At the bottom of AppRoach, you'll find the menu bar. This menu will stay with you, no matter where in your AppRoach you go, and you can always use it to navigate around the main features.

Your menu features four elements: the home icon to take you back to this home page, the driving icon to integrate with your map application of choice, the walking icon to take you to your approach, and your profile icon to take you to your personal settings.
