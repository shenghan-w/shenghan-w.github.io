Approaching with AppRoach

If you've signed up and loaded in all of your preferences, there should a list of the most popular climbing crags already loaded up on the landing page of AppRoach.

Let's take a look at the process of approaching a climb, all within AppRoach.

Steps to Your Approach

  • Selecting Your Trail
  • Videos and Waypoints
  • Reporting Inaccurate Information

Selecting Your Trail

If you're ready to approach your climb, let's get started with selecting a climb or a trail! You'll find the most popular ones for your area already populated on your homescreen, but if you can't find the trail you're looking for, hit the search icon, and type in your trail name or climb name.

After selecting your trail, your map will appear! AppRoach is linked to GPS and tracks your progress so you never get lost.

The key feature of AppRoach is in our user-uploaded waypoints.

Videos and Waypoints

We know that climbing trails aren't given professional upkeep, so sometimes it can be hard to know you're on the right path. Clicking on any of our waypoints will zoom in and allow you to view a short video, letting you follow along and match up your surroundings as you hike!

The map will remain on screen for guidance, and on the top right, the picture of your next waypoint will load. Clicking on that icon will start the next video in your chain of waypoints.

Finally, remember if you ever toggle out of your navigation, one click on the menu bar will get you back to your map and waypoints.
